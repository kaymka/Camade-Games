Our Commitment to Canadian Players

At Northern Arena, we believe gaming should be fun, safe, and balanced. Although we operate with virtual currency only (no real-money gambling), we are committed to promoting healthy gaming habits in accordance with Canadian responsible gaming guidelines.

Know the Signs of Problem Gaming

Ask yourself these questions honestly:

⏰

Do you spend more time gaming than you intended?

📵

Do you neglect work, school, or relationships to play?

😤

Do you feel irritable or anxious when not playing?

🔄

Have you tried to cut back but couldn't?

😴

Do you lose sleep because of gaming?

💭

Do you think about gaming during other activities?

If you answered "yes" to several questions, you may want to seek support.

Self-Exclusion Program

If you need a break from gaming, you can request self-exclusion:

  • Email rg@northernarena.ca with subject "Self-Exclusion Request"
  • Choose duration: 30, 90, or 180 days
  • During exclusion: Cannot access games or earn/use Maple Coins
  • After exclusion period ends: Access is automatically restored
🛡️
Self-exclusion requests are processed within 24 hours. All requests are confidential.

Parental Controls

We recommend using parental control software to manage children's online gaming:

  • Qustodio — Cross-platform parental controls
  • Net Nanny — Content filtering and time limits
  • Apple Screen Time — Built-in iOS controls
  • Google Family Link — Android device management

Northern Arena is intended for users aged 18+ only.
