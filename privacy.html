Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2025

📅 Effective: April 15, 2025 ⚖️ PIPEDA Compliant 🇨🇦 Toronto, Canada

1. Information We Collect

At Northern Arena ("we", "us", "our"), we respect your privacy. Since our platform allows anonymous gameplay without registration, we collect minimal personal data. Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), we are transparent about our data practices.

📋 We may collect:

  • Technical data: IP address (anonymised), browser type, device information, and gameplay statistics
  • Cookie data: Preferences and session information to improve your experience
  • Voluntary information: If you contact us via email or contact form, we store your message and contact details
  • Gameplay data: Win/loss records, coin balances, and achievement progress

🍁 No registration required: You never need to provide your name, email address, or any identifying information to play our games.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely for legitimate business purposes under PIPEDA Section 7:

  • Providing and maintaining our browser games
  • Calculating virtual Maple Coins and leaderboards
  • Improving platform performance and user experience for Canadian players
  • Responding to support inquiries (voluntary data only)
  • Preventing fraud, cheating, and ensuring fair play
  • Analysing game popularity to improve our library

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use essential cookies for gameplay functionality and analytics cookies to understand how players use our platform. For detailed information, please review our Cookie Policy.

🍪
Cookie Consent

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. However, some game features may not function properly without essential cookies.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. Limited data may be shared with:

  • Service providers: Hosting, analytics, and cloud services under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities: If required by Canadian law, court order, or as permitted under PIPEDA
  • Payment processors: (Not applicable — we use virtual currency only, no real money transactions)

All third-party providers are located in Canada or have equivalent privacy protections under Canadian law.

5. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

As a user in Canada, you have the following rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act:

🔍

Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal data

✏️

Right to Rectify

Correct inaccurate information

🗑️

Right to Delete

Request deletion of your data

🛡️

Right to Withdraw

Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@northernarena.ca. Since we don't require registration, most gameplay data is anonymised and cannot be linked back to you personally.

6. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning
  • Access controls and authentication for staff
  • Data retention period: 24 months for anonymised gameplay data

In the event of a data breach affecting Canadian residents, we will notify the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (OPC) and affected individuals as required by law.
