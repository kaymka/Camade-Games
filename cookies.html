What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit websites. They help us remember your preferences, improve your gaming experience, and understand how players interact with Northern Arena.

🍁
Canadian Privacy Standards

We follow the guidance of the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (OPC) regarding cookie consent and transparency.

Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
northern_session Maintains your game session and coin balance Session
northern_preferences Stores your game settings and language preference 1 year
northern_analytics Anonymous usage data for platform improvement 2 years
northern_coins Tracks your Maple Coins balance (guest mode) Persistent

Third-Party Cookies

We use limited third-party services that may place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymised traffic analysis (IP anonymisation enabled for Canadian users)
  • Cloudflare: Security and performance optimization
  • Game providers: Embedded game content may use their own cookies

We do not control third-party cookies. Please review their respective privacy policies for more information.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

🌐 Browser Settings:

Chrome Firefox Safari Edge Brave

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View all cookies stored on your device
  • Delete individual or all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites
⚠️
Important: Disabling essential cookies may prevent games from loading properly and reset your Maple Coins balance.

Analytics & Tracking

We use Google Analytics with IP anonymisation enabled to protect your privacy. This means your full IP address is never stored or shared. We collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Game popularity and play counts
  • Device type and browser information
  • Geographic region (country/city level, anonymised)

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
